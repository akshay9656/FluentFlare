FAQ

Everything You Need to Know: Common Questions and Detailed Answers

What is the 40-day training program, and how does it work?

The 40-day training program is designed to improve your English speaking skills through daily tasks. Each day, you'll be assigned tasks such as reading, recording audio, writing reviews, and grammar tests. Your progress will be tracked, and you'll receive feedback from AI to help you improve.

How are the audio recordings reviewed, and what kind of feedback will I receive?

When you upload an audio recording, it will be reviewed by an AI, which will provide feedback on your pronunciation, fluency, and grammar. The feedback will help you understand your strengths and areas for improvement.

Can I pause or skip days in the 40-day training program?

The program is designed to be completed over 40 consecutive days to ensure steady progress. However, if you need to skip a day or take a break, you can resume where you left off. Just keep in mind that this may affect your overall progress and feedback.

How can I track my progress throughout the 40-day program?

Your progress is tracked through a progress bar that shows your completion of daily tasks over the 40-day period. You can view your progress on the "Dashboard" page, where you'll see how many tasks you've completed and how much of the program you've finished.

Is there a way to contact support if I have technical issues or questions?

Yes, if you have any technical issues or questions, you can contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page. Provide as much detail as possible about your issue, and our team will assist you in resolving it.
